## Pricing Strategy

Per Wes's brief, activities should be:
- **Free or low-cost** for basic (mini golf, campfire, jungle cinema)
- **Mid-range** for guided (yoga, axe throwing, survival course)
- **Premium** for private (romantic dinner, floating breakfast, ice bath)

**Goal:** activities are not profit centers — they're **engagement + differentiation** that drives room bookings + social media.
