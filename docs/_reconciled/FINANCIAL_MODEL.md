## 7. Build Approach (3-year phase)

| Year | Capex € | Deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| Year 1 (35%) | 1,926,308 | Basic infra (water/electric/roads) + 5 first cabins + reception |
| Year 2 (35%) | 1,926,308 | Restaurant + natural pool + 10 more cabins (Year-2 batch) |
| Year 3 (30%) | 1,651,120 | Final 15 cabins + wellness + activities |

**Build sequence notes:**
- Build only in **dry season** (May-Oct) for cob/earthen work
- 3-5 month build window per cabin batch
- 6 different styles across the 30 cabins so guests can return for new experiences
- 3 Bali craftsmen + 5-8 PY workers as the core build team

## 9. Investment Vehicle

**Per Wes's working files (`invesment center.docx`):**
- Founder-controlled structure (you keep absolute decision control)
- 3-5 investors get capital + co-ownership
- Nobody can remove the founder or take over the company
- Strategy: raise capital at 10-11%, lend at 15-30% to projects
- Sweet spot: real estate financing (15-20%, secured) + project loans (18-25%)
- **Warning:** Don't lend too cheap (10% in, 12% out = bankruptcy at first problem)
