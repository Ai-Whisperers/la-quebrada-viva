## What the contract does NOT say (gaps)

- No cláusula de **posesión anticipada** — buyers don't take possession until escritura signs.
- No **Anexo I** in the borrador — the technical descriptions of each finca (linderos, rumbos, medidas) are referenced as "Anexo I" but not included in the borrador on hand. Needs to be obtained from sellers' escritura pública titles.
- No **plazo de entrega material** beyond the escritura signing.
- No **insurance clause** for the property between boleto and escritura.
- No **tax responsibility allocation** beyond the impuesto a la renta — IVA on commission? Honorarios? (Typically buyer pays notarial, seller pays impuesto a la renta — verify with Peña.)
- No clause on **what happens to the buildings/edificaciones** on the land ("con todo lo en ellos edificado, clavado, plantado y adherido" is mentioned in Cl. PRIMERA but no inventory of what exists).
- No **mediation/arbitration clause** — disputes go straight to tribunals of Asunción (Cl. DÉCIMA PRIMERA).
