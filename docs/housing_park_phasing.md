# Housing Park Phasing — 5-Year Build-Out of the 62-ha Property

**Status:** living document. Sequences the 8 typologies + restaurant + amenities on Wesley's 62 ha, from escritura (2026-06-27) through year-5 stabilisation (2031). Couples to `lqv/typologies/__init__.py`, `lqv/restaurant/*`, `lqv/amenities/*`.

The goal is to spread capital and labour so each phase generates revenue or de-risks the next, and so the property never looks like a half-built construction zone.

## Strategic principle

The cob/bottle LQV house (typology 1) is BOTH:
- the founder's residence + workshop,
- and the **show home** that prospective housing-park buyers tour to decide.

So LQV-1 must finish first. No other typology can predate it. Period.

## Phase 0 — Escritura + entitlements (months 0–3, 2026-Q3)

- 2026-06-27: escritura traslativa signed. Day-by-day T-7 / T-5 / T-2 / signing-day / T+30 checklist + risk register: [`CLOSING_DAY_PREP.md`](./CLOSING_DAY_PREP.md).
- 2026-07: SET RUC + facturación electrónica registered (Wesley as sole prop initially; SRL formed later if needed).
- 2026-07: arquitecto colegiado retained, anteproyecto submitted to Escobar municipality.
- 2026-08: surveyor mensura completed (`docs/site_data_spike.md` Tier 1).
- 2026-09: anteproyecto approved; environmental licence (Ley 294) if required for housing park.

**Capital required:** ~USD 8 000 (soft costs).
**Revenue:** zero.

## Phase 1 — LQV house + entry road (months 4–14, 2026-Q4 to 2027-Q3)

- 2026-10: clearing, foundation, trees felled.
- 2026-11 to 2027-04: cob walls + roof (Phase 0–6 in `docs/build_sequence.md`).
- 2027-05 to 2027-07: services + tatakuá + finish.
- 2027-07: entry road graded (3 m wide, gravel, drainage), 800 m from RN-1 spur.
- 2027-08: habilitación; Wesley + family move in.

**Capital required:** ~USD 56 000 (per `docs/bom.md`).
**Revenue:** zero (Wesley primary residence). Optional Airbnb in shoulder weeks if SENATUR alta is filed.

## Phase 2 — Restaurant + dining hall (months 12–22, 2027-Q3 to 2028-Q2)

- 2027-08: restaurant pad cleared, 300 m southwest of LQV house, near the entry road.
- 2027-09 to 2028-02: dining hall built (sod-roof extension of LQV language; serves 40 covers).
- 2028-03 to 2028-05: outdoor kitchen + dual tatakuá + commercial kitchen rough-in.
- 2028-06: SENATUR + SET licensing; soft open.

**Capital required:** ~USD 90 000.
**Revenue (year 1):** USD 30 000–60 000 net (Asunción weekend trade; pacha-mama farm-to-table positioning).

**Key:** restaurant must finish BEFORE typology bookings, because it's the destination that justifies the trip from Asunción.

## Phase 3 — Amenities (months 18–30, 2028-Q1 to 2028-Q4)

- 2028-Q1: spring trail + viewpoint deck at the upper cliff.
- 2028-Q2: stream pool deck + footbridge upgraded (footbridge already complete).
- 2028-Q3: forest schoolroom / yoga deck on the upper terrace.
- 2028-Q4: small stargazing platform on the cerro shoulder.

**Capital required:** ~USD 35 000.
**Revenue:** packaged with restaurant + housing-park (incremental).

## Phase 4 — Typology 2 (Bamboo Tropical Modern), pilot housing unit (months 24–34, 2028-Q3 to 2029-Q2)

- 2028-Q3: pad clearing, foundation.
- 2028-Q4 to 2029-Q1: bamboo + thatch construction.
- 2029-Q2: services, finishes, habilitación.

**Capital required:** ~USD 70 000.
**Revenue:** vacation rental USD 90–130/night; year-1 estimate USD 18 000 net.

**Why typology 2 here:** European tourists love the bamboo aesthetic AND it's the fastest-to-build of the 8 typologies, so it de-risks the model.

## Phase 5 — Typology 3 (Adobe Courtyard) (months 30–42, 2029-Q1 to 2029-Q4)

## Phase 6 — Typologies 4 & 5 in parallel (months 36–54, 2029-Q3 to 2031-Q1)

**Revenue:** combined USD 60 000+ net year-1.

## Phase 7 — Typologies 6, 7, 8 (months 50–66, 2030-Q4 to 2032-Q2)

Final 3 typologies (selected from): Tropical Modernist, Earth-bag Dome, Container Modular, Net-zero Hempcrete, Off-grid Yurt.

- Phase rolling — one starts per quarter for 4 quarters.
- 2032-Q2: full 8 typologies operational.

**Capital required:** ~USD 250 000 combined.
**Revenue:** combined USD 90 000+ net year-1.

## Risks

1. **Sandy soil at typology 6 / 7 pad.** Survey to confirm (Phase 0 deliverable).
2. **Lapacho FSC market price spike.** Pre-buy lapacho during 2026-Q4 dip.
3. **Currency risk** (USD vs PYG). Maintain operating buffer in PYG; capex purchases mid-year in USD.
4. **Permit delays** at Escobar municipality. Escalation plan: arquitecto colegiado on retainer.
5. **Cob mason scarcity.** Train 2 local masons during Phase 1 to scale into Phase 5–7.
6. **Off-grid limits** (water, PV) at high occupancy. Solve per typology — each has own services.
