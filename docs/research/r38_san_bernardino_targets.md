## 0 · Why San Bernardino at all

San Bernardino (department of Cordillera, 49 km east of Asunción, on the west shore of Lago Ypacaraí) is the historical summer-migration destination of Asunción's German-Paraguayan community. The pattern dates to the founding of the town in **1881** by German colonists from Hamburg and Westfalen — a continuous 144-year cultural corridor that today still anchors:

- The wealthier slice of Asunción's German-Paraguayan upper-middle class (lake houses, beach clubs, summer regattas).
- The Goethe alumni / AHK PY (German-Paraguayan Chamber of Commerce) social network.
- A small but persistent gastronomic ecosystem — German bakeries, sausage houses, brewery clubs — that overlaps tightly with LQV's "German-Paraguayan kitchen" angle.

**For LQV this matters because:**

1. **Year-round domestic shoulder.** December-February is San Ber's peak. May-September is its valley. Mbopicua sits ~210 km east of San Ber — a long weekend distance, not a flight. We can position LQV as the "extended-stay forest counterpart" to a weekend in San Ber.
2. **Bilingual staff pipeline.** Service staff trained at San Ber hotels/clubs already have DE/ES (and often EN) — recruitable upward.
3. **Cultural fit for the kitchen angle.** A San Ber-trained chef de cuisine on the German-Paraguayan side is a natural anchor for the LQV restaurant.
4. **Referral pipe.** A San Ber family with the means to spend on a 4-cabin Phase-1 stay is a likely first-tier domestic customer.

## 5 · What we do NOT pitch (yet)

- **No outreach to Awasi-tier R&C properties on the San Bernardino side.** That conversation belongs to the R37 track (Awasi Iguazú), not here. R&C exclusivity overlaps cut across the lake region too.
- **No direct social-media campaigns aimed at San Ber retail demand.** Phase 1 is 4 cabins. We do not need volume — we need the right 200 households of awareness.
- **No event sponsorships above 4 figures USD/year** until Phase 1 has a confirmed opening date and a booking system live.
- **No discounting offered to clubs** as a closing tool. Position is "members invited to a preview night," not "members get 20 % off."
