## Risk register — watch for these on signing day

| Risk | Trigger | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| Seller no-show or refusal | Sellers don't appear or refuse to sign | Cl. NOVENA: sellers owe **double seña = G. 500.600.000** to buyers. Document with Peña + retain attorney. |
| Buyer no-show or non-payment | Buyers don't appear or funds not ready | Cl. NOVENA: buyers forfeit seña G. 250.300.000 (less notary costs already incurred). Avoid by confirming funds T-5. |
| Last-minute gravamen / embargo / inhibición | Surfaced by certificados catastrales | **Do NOT sign**. Invoke Cl. NOVENA prórroga automática for administrative delays. Sellers must clear before signing. |
| Anexo I still missing | Sellers never delivered the technical descriptions | Peña drafts from the títulos de propiedad — slows signing but does not block. Chase NOW, not on signing day. |
| Cl. OCTAVA (ii) docs not delivered | Sellers haven't given títulos / planos / informes periciales / comprobantes de impuestos | Chase via Peña T-7 minimum. If still missing T-2, escalate to attorney — sellers in default per Cl. SÉPTIMA / Cl. OCTAVA. |
| Price escalation attempt | Sellers try to raise price between boleto and escritura | Cl. SEGUNDA: price is **fixed at G. 2.503.000.000**. No clause permits adjustment. Refuse. |
| IVA / impuestos confusion | Disagreement on who pays what | Cl. SÉPTIMA: sellers pay impuesto a la renta + impuestos inmobiliarios al día. Buyers typically pay honorarios notariales + IVA on commission. Confirm with Peña T-7. |
| Cheque rejected / wire delayed | Funds not in escribanía's account on signing day | **Do NOT sign without funds confirmed** — risk forfeiture per Cl. NOVENA. Reschedule via prórroga if needed. |

## Things that are NOT in the contract — be aware

- **No posesión anticipada** — buyers cannot enter as owners until escritura signs.
- **No insurance clause** for the period between boleto and escritura. If a building burns or a tree falls in this window, the legal situation is murky. Verify what the sellers carried.
- **No mediation / arbitration clause** — disputes go straight to tribunals of Asunción (Cl. DÉCIMA PRIMERA). Slow + expensive. Avoid escalation.
- **No inventory of edificaciones** — "con todo lo en ellos edificado, clavado, plantado y adherido" (Cl. PRIMERA) but no list. Walk the site post-signing with photo survey to document what's there.
