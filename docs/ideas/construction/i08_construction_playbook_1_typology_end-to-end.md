## What it is

A 30-page PDF that documents how to build one Type A house end-to-end: site prep, foundation, walls, roof, utilities, finishes. With photos, materials lists, timing, costs.

## Why this matters

Without a documented playbook, every house is rebuilt-from-scratch in Wes's head. New contractors re-learn every step. A playbook turns the typology into a product Wes can scale or sell.
