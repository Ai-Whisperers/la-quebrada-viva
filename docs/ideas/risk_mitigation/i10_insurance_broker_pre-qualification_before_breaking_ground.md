## What it is

Before any structure goes up, get 3 insurance broker quotes confirming fire + storm + liability coverage is available at <10% of project value. Hard gate.

## Why this matters

Building wooden/bamboo houses without confirmed insurance is gambling. Either insurance is affordable -> proceed. Or it's not -> switch to cob or smaller scope.
