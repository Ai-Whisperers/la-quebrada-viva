## What it is

A safari tent on a raised wooden deck, 25-40 m2, real bed, private bathroom with bucket-shower or compost toilet. Phase 1 revenue generator that's cheaper and faster than Type A cob.

## Why this matters

Wes's catalog jumps from "land + buildings" straight to Type A cob houses. There's a missing rung: tent-based lodging that gets first paying guest in 3 months, not 12. Validates the booking pipeline, guest experience, and pricing without committing to the full typology build.

## Cost (estimate)

$8-15k per tent including deck + bathroom. $30-60k for 4-tent pilot

## Deliverable

First 2-4 tents operational + bookable within 90 days

## Done = shipped

First guest paying $80-150/night in glamping tents
