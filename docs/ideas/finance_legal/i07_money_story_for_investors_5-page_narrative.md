## What it is

A 5-page written document that explains: where the money goes, what investors get back, in what order, with what risk. Not a deck. A written story.

## Why this matters

Decks sell fantasies. Stories sell decisions. European investors (esp. NL) respond to written narrative + numbers, not slides. Wes's target investor profile is 40-65 year old, mid-six-figures, looking for lifestyle + modest return. That audience reads.
