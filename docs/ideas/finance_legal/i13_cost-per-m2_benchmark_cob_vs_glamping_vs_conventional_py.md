## Why this matters

Wes's catalog talks about cob + bamboo + glamping + conventional as if they're equally easy. They're not. The cob house costs ~$225/m2 (per the NL price doc). A glamping tent is closer to $300/m2 but ships in 90 days. Conventional PY is $300-400/m2 but takes 9-12 months. Without the benchmark, decision paralysis.
