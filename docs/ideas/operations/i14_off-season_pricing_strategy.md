## What it is

PY has wet (Nov-Mar) and dry (May-Oct) seasons. European traveler demand has its own seasonality. Map both, then define 3 pricing tiers: peak / shoulder / off.

## Why this matters

Wes's catalog assumes steady revenue. It's not steady. Off-season pricing (lower) keeps the operation alive when bookings drop. Peak pricing (higher) captures the European Christmas + Easter + summer rush.
