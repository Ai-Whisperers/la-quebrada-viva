## What it is

A single moestuin bed (already on the roadmap as A08) but specifically scoped to a pilot weekend: invite 1 chef + 6 guests for a Friday-Sunday "harvest and cook" experience. Validates the moestuin + chef pipeline.

## Why this matters

Moestuin as standalone idea is too soft. A specific weekend format makes it operational. The chef-pipeline also opens the door to the Phase 3 Dutch-German restaurant (A06).
